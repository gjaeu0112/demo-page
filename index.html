Drinking coffee reduces the risk of depression by almost one-third. The effect of reducing depression is known to be related to the anti-inflammatory characteristics of coffee. Some antidepressants in coffee lower the level of inflammation in the body to relieve depression. Coffee also contains phytochemicals, which are food for beneficial bacteria in the intestine. Beneficial bacteria in the intestine can act on the brain and produce other compounds that make you feel better.
